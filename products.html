STAINLESS STEEL SHEETS/COIL

Stainless Steel sheet/coil is flexible and utilized in various applications.These sheets are used in construction, food administration applications, transportation, substance, marine, oil tanks, boilers and material enterprises. S.S coils (0.4,0.5mm) are used in “Cladding”. Cladding is a new trend that mostly many industries are following specially the textile sector. Cladding is basically the new skin applied on other material such as pipes so that the appearance of the building looks good and it also provides degree of thermal insulation.

Grade:304/304L, 316/316L, 310

Size Range:4 x 8, 5 x 10, 4 X 10 ,1000mm x 2000mm, 2000mm x 4000mm

Thickness:0.4mm to 50mm
