BUTT WELD FITTINGS

Butt weld pipe fittings are welded from pipes that allow the change in direction of flows, reducing the pipe size, close or end the pipe line and diversifying the pipe line in any industry. They are available in various forms some of them includes elbows, tee, end caps and reducer etc. These fittings have various nominal pipe sizes and wall thickness depending on the pressure of the pipe line.

Type:Welded and Seamless

BSP THREADED FITTINGS

BSP stands for “British standard pipe”; it has thread on their ends and could be inside thread or outside thread as well. This is used in interconnecting pipe lines, sanitary works or in other industrial projects. It is often used where there are low pressures. Maintenance in such pipe lines connected with threaded fittings is usually easy then butt weld fittings. They are used in wide range of industries such as oil gas, chemical and petroleum etc. They are in various forms such as elbows, tee, pipe nipples, barrel nipple, union, bushes and sockets etc.

Stainless Steel:304/ 304L/316/ 316L

Size: ¼” to 4”

Pressure Rating:150lb, 300lb
