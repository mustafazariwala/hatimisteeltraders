STAINLESS STEEL FLANGES

A Flange is a technique for interfacing pipes, valves, pumps and other equipment to form a piping system. It is primarily used where a connecting or dismantling joint is needed. These joints functions for connecting a pipe to fittings, valves, mechanical equipment, or any other necessary integral components within a piping line. Additionally, it gives a simple access to cleaning, examination or alteration. They are typically welded or screwed.
