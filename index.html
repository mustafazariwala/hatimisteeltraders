About Us

Twenty years of Hatimi Steel Traders is a glorified journey that is bestowed with great success and development. Starting a new venture in the competitive market was a challenge we dealt with courage and honesty. Since the start we have had pre-determined goals to make us standout. With sheer commitment and immense dedication, we were able to successfully cultivate our business. The unending endeavors made us capable enough to deal with valuable projects and tenders. We are known for our exceptional services and unmatchable standards because we ensure not to compromise on anything when it comes to quality. Today, Hatimi Steel traders has become one of the leaders in Pakistan. Our aim is to excel in our field of business whilst building indestructible bonds with our clients.
